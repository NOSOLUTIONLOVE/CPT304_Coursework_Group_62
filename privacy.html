1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the UNIQLO Inventory Management System handles information when you use this web application. We are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

This inventory system is a local web application that does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All inventory data you create or import is stored locally in your browser's memory and is not shared with any third party.

3. Use of Cookies

This website uses only functional cookies that are essential for the operation of the inventory management system. These cookies do not track your browsing activity or collect personal information. Specifically:

  • Language Preference Cookie: Stores your preferred display language (English or Chinese) to provide a consistent user experience across visits.
  • Cookie Consent Cookie: Records your consent choice regarding cookie usage, so the cookie banner is not displayed repeatedly.

No advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies are used on this website.

4. Data Storage

All inventory data that you load, create, or modify is stored in your browser's local storage and will persist across sessions. You can export your data at any time using the "Save Data" function, which downloads the data as a local file to your device. To remove stored data, you can clear your browser's local storage.

5. Third-Party Services

This application loads fonts from Google Fonts and uses Bootstrap and DataTables libraries delivered via CDN. These third-party services may collect standard web server log information. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies for more details.

6. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected by this application, there is no personal data to access, rectify, or delete. You have full control over your inventory data through the application's interface.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact your system administrator.

1. 引言

本隐私政策说明了 UNIQLO 库存管理系统在您使用此 Web 应用程序时如何处理信息。我们致力于保持数据实践的透明度。

2. 我们收集的信息

本库存系统是一个本地 Web 应用程序，不会收集、存储或传输任何个人数据至外部服务器。您创建或导入的所有库存数据均存储在浏览器的内存中，不会与任何第三方共享。

3. Cookie 的使用

本网站仅使用对库存管理系统运行至关重要的功能性 cookie。这些 cookie 不会跟踪您的浏览活动或收集个人信息。具体包括：

  • 语言偏好 Cookie：存储您偏好的显示语言（英文或中文），以便在多次访问中提供一致的用户体验。
  • Cookie 同意 Cookie：记录您对 cookie 使用的同意选择，以便不再重复显示 cookie 横幅。

本网站不使用任何广告、分析或跟踪类 cookie。

4. 数据存储

您加载、创建或修改的所有库存数据均存储在**浏览器的本地存储**中，数据在会话结束后仍会保留。您可以随时使用「保存数据」功能将数据导出为本地文件，或通过清除浏览器数据来删除已存储的库存记录。

5. 第三方服务

本应用程序通过 CDN 加载 Google Fonts 字体，并使用 Bootstrap 和 DataTables 库。这些第三方服务可能会收集标准的 Web 服务器日志信息。我们建议您查阅其各自的隐私政策以获取更多详情。

6. 您的权利

由于本应用程序不收集任何个人数据，因此没有个人数据可供访问、更正或删除。您可以通过应用程序界面完全控制您的库存数据。

7. 本政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何变更将反映在此页面上。我们建议您定期查看本政策。

8. 联系方式

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系您的系统管理员。
